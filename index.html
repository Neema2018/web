ABOUT ZERO SEVEN

The domestic service application aims at creating a free platform for the largest domestic services supply and demand in communities near you. It is simply going to act as a bridge connecting service providers and consumers. A research carried out by Ipsos on August 2018 shows that about 45% of Kenyans are self-employed whereas 12% of the population is unemployed. Only about 11% are employed in the private sector. According to the survey, main source of self-employment are foodstuffs kiosk, agriculture produce marketing, general goods kiosk, livestock marketing, boutique, hairdressing, transport, builder and in the mechanical repairs. (Information extracted from the Ipsos survey) The idea is to advertise this self employed population that administers domestic services so that it is easier for them to reach potential consumers.
